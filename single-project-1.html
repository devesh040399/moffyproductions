About

Actor Swara Bhasker is all set to play an investigative officer yet again in the upcoming murder mystery ‘Mimamsa’. While a lot is not known about the film, sources have it that Swara's ‘Mimamsa’ will be talking about various pandemics that have ever struck the world, including Swine Flu, SARS, and more recently, Coronavirus. And now that it is out in the open, it has only added more curiosity as to how the makers will incorporate the subject in the film and what angle they will explore. Given the fact that details about ‘Mimamsa’ have been kept under wraps for the longest time, this update is definitely proof that the movie is going to be a game-changer for the genre that it deals with

The Vision

Produced by Moffy Production and co-produced by K.P production, the film assures edge-of-the-seat drama to its audiences. Directed by Gagan Puri and written by Arpan Dev, the film will also feature Bijendra Kala in an important role.
